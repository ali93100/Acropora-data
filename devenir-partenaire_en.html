Become a Partner

Join a specialized MDM & Master Data ecosystem

Why collaborate with us

🗄️

Access to our MDM platform

Benefit from demos, sandbox environments, and accelerators for your prospects and clients.

📣

Co-marketing & Visibility

Webinars, events, co-branded case studies, and presence on our communication channels.

Access to qualified leads

Qualified opportunities from our campaigns and our multi-sector client base.

Our Differentiation

Scalable repositories, 99.9% data quality, and a proven methodology on 200+ projects.

Partnership Types

Technology Partner

Integrate your solutions with our MDM platform and build joint high-value offers.

  • Certified Connectors & APIs
  • Shared Product Roadmaps
  • Dedicated Technical Sessions

Business Partner

Resell our MDM solutions to your customers, with structured pre-sales support.

  • Packaged Offers & Pricing Grids
  • Pre-sales Support & RFPs
  • Incentive Programs

Integrator Partner

Support the deployment and industrialization of MDM repositories for our mutual clients.

  • Access to our Project Methodology
  • Coaching on Initial Missions
  • Library of Templates & Best Practices

Partnership Levels

Silver

Partner portal access, dedicated documentation, initial training, and standard support.

Gold

All Silver + co-marketing, certification training, dedicated account manager, and quarterly reviews.

Platinum

All Gold + joint development, systematic lead sharing, and preferential commercial conditions.

Requirements

Data Experience

Proven expertise in ERP, CRM, business IS environments, or large-scale data projects.

Technical Certifications

Vendor and cloud certifications appreciated (Informatica, Snowflake, cloud hyperscalers, etc.).

Business Capability

Ability to generate opportunities, manage a sales pipe, and support clients over the long term.
