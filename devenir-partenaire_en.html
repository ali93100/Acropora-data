PARTNERSHIP TYPES

Choose the model that fits you

Technology

Technology Partner

For software publishers and data solution providers who want to integrate or certify within the Acropora ecosystem.

  • Certified technical integration
  • Co-development of connectors
  • Access to the Acropora innovation lab
  • Listed in our RFPs
Business

Business Partner

For IT service companies, integrators, consultancies, and business introducers who want to enrich their offering with Acropora's MDM expertise.

  • Commission on business referrals
  • Co-marketing and co-selling
  • Training and certification
  • Dedicated pre-sales support
Strategic

Strategic Alliance

For players who want a deep alliance relationship: joint go-to-market, mutual commitments, and a shared vision.

  • Formalized partnership agreement
  • Contractualized common objectives
  • Priority access to leadership teams
  • Co-innovation and shared R&D
SELECTION CRITERIA

What we look for in a partner

🎯

Strategic alignment

Your vision and target markets must be compatible with our MDM and data governance positioning.

💡

Complementary offerings

We favor partners whose solutions or services complement our offering without replacing it — synergy takes precedence over competition.

🏅

Recognized expertise

Proven experience in your field, solid client references, and a competent team are fundamental prerequisites.

🤝

Long-term commitment

We build lasting relationships. We look for partners who embrace a long-term, non-transactional partnership approach.

🔒

Ethical standards

GDPR compliance, good industry practices, transparency in client relationships — our partners share our ethical values.

📍

Geographic presence

A local presence (Mauritius, Sub-Saharan Africa, Europe, Middle East) is an asset for developing complementary markets.
