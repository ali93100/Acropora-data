STRUCTURED METHODOLOGY

From ambition
to executable roadmap

A data project without solid scoping is like a construction site without a blueprint. At Acropora Data, every engagement begins with a rigorous scoping phase that defines the actual perimeter, risks, and expected outcomes.

Our scoping methodology reduces the risk of budget overruns by 3× and compresses the time between kickoff and first delivery by an average of 40%.

OUR 5-STEP PROCESS

A structured path to clarity

01

Discovery Audit

360° inventory of your existing data landscape, tools, and processes.

02

Data Mapping

Identification and documentation of all critical data flows and master data domains.

03

Scope Definition

Precise perimeter for the project: included, excluded, phased — no ambiguity.

04

Roadmap

Sequenced delivery plan with milestones, KPIs, resource requirements, and budget.

05

Stakeholder Validation

Formal sign-off process with all stakeholders before implementation begins.

KEY DELIVERABLES

Concrete outputs from every scoping engagement

🗺️

Data Cartography Document

A complete map of your data landscape — systems, flows, ownership, quality levels, and interdependencies. The foundation for all subsequent decisions.

📋

Prioritized Project Charter

A structured charter defining objectives, success criteria, constraints, assumptions, and a prioritized backlog of initiatives.

🛣️

Executive Roadmap

A sequenced, budgeted delivery roadmap with clear phases, go/no-go gates, and business value milestones for executive stakeholders.

⚠️

Risk & Dependency Register

An exhaustive register of technical, organizational, and regulatory risks with mitigation plans and dependency mapping across initiatives.
