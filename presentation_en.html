Overview

Our history, our vision since 2023

Presentation

ACROPORA DATA was founded by data professionals with extensive experience in global projects. Our agile and flexible approach allows us to deliver high-quality services tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

Based in Mauritius, we offer a strategic advantage in terms of time zone alignment for both European and Asian markets, combined with competitive pricing and bilingual teams proficient in both English and French.

Who are we?

ACROPORA helps organizations take control of their data-not just by implementing tools, but by making data actually usable, reliable and business-ready.

ACROPORA brings a different approach :

- Less theory
- More execution
- More impact
