Tool Comparison

Selenium · UiPath · Tosca

Choose the right tool based on your project context

Open source
Selenium
Technical Profile
Code-oriented

Requires scripting (Java, Python, JS…). Total control over test logic.

Static Data

Datasets are defined in scripts. No native dynamic injection.

Individual Usage

No native collaborative platform. Ideal for a developer or small team.

Low-code RPA
UiPath
Agile Teams
Application Scan

Automatically detects and maps UI elements. Low-code requirement.

Data from files

Import Excel, CSV, and API. Dynamic data injection into test scenarios.

Collaborative Environment

Shared platform for teams. Integrated role and access management.

Enterprise
Tosca
Enterprise
Advanced Scan (MBT)

Model-based automation. Full coverage, no manual scripting.

Contextual Data

Manage data directly in the application. Context-based isolation and control.

Reports & Case Generation

Automatic test case generation and lifecycle reporting integrated into the quality pipeline.

Choose Selenium if…
Your team is technical, you want maximum control, and have fewer budget constraints on licenses.
Choose UiPath if…
You want to accelerate automation with low-code and have mixed teams (business + IT).
Choose Tosca if…
You manage complex enterprise projects with high governance and quality reporting requirements.
